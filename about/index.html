데이터와 원칙

  • 코스 데이터는 공공데이터포털(data.go.kr)의 공공·오픈 라이선스 데이터를 기반으로 하며, 출처를 각 페이지에 표기합니다.
  • 업로드한 GPX 파일은 브라우저 안에서만 처리되어 서버에 저장되지 않습니다.
  • 도구는 무료로 유지되며, 운영은 광고(Google AdSense)와 제휴 링크(쿠팡 파트너스)로 지원됩니다. 자세한 내용은 개인정보처리방침을 참고하세요.

Race Planner (raceplanner.online) is a set of free tools for runners, trail runners, and HYROX athletes. No sign-up, no cost beyond ads — anyone can use it.

What you can do

Route Poster Generator

Turn a GPX, Strava activity, or race course into a framable running-route poster and save it as a PNG.

Trail Course Library

Browse real Korean courses — 100 Famous Mountains, national forest trails, dulle-gil loops — with terrain maps and elevation.

HYROX Finish Calculator

Get target run pace and roxzone budget for your goal finish time.

Marathon Pace & Predictor

Split-by-split pace and finish prediction for your goal time.

Data and principles

  • Course data is based on public / open-license data from Korea's data.go.kr, with sources credited on each page.
  • GPX files you upload are processed in your browser only and never stored on our servers.
  • The tools stay free; the site is supported by ads (Google AdSense) and affiliate links (Coupang Partners). See our Privacy Policy for details.

Feedback

Report bugs, request a course, or reach out about partnerships via our Contact page. Our goal is to build tools runners actually find useful.
