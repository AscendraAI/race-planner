How is pace calculated?

Pace is simply time divided by distance. This tool also converts it to min/mile and km/h, then breaks your race into per-kilometer splits so you can plan an even effort from the gun.

How does the predictor work?

It uses Riegel's formula: T2 = T1 × (D2 / D1)^1.06. It's a well-known endurance model — accurate for similar effort levels, less so when jumping from very short to very long distances without specific training.
