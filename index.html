FAQ

Where do I get a GPX file?
Export from Strava (a run → ⋯ → Export GPX), Garmin Connect, Coros, Apple Health apps, or any activity that records a track.
Is my data uploaded anywhere?
No. Parsing and rendering happen entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
Can I print it?
Yes — the PNG is 1080px and looks great on social. High-resolution print files are coming soon.
